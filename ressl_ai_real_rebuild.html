FAQs

Got questions?
We've got answers.

Still have questions?
Contact us and we'll help you out.

01 How long does it take to get set up?
Most businesses are up and running within a week. We handle the entire setup — you just walk us through how your office works today.
02 Do I need to replace my existing software?
No. Ressl works on top of the tools you already use — ServiceTitan, CCCOne, Jobber, QuickBooks, whatever you have. Nothing gets ripped out.
03 What if the AI makes a mistake?
You set the rules for what the AI can and can't do on its own. Anything outside those boundaries gets flagged for a human to review before anything happens.
04 Do I need to hire someone to manage the AI?
No. That's the point. Your existing team stays in control — they just stop doing the repetitive stuff.
05 Is my customer and job data secure?
Yes. Your data stays in your environment. We don't store sensitive customer or job information on our end.
06 What kinds of businesses is this built for?
Any trades or home services business with an office doing repetitive work — HVAC, plumbing, electrical, collision repair, roofing, landscaping, and more.
